CHANNEL IDEA #1

Curated "Deep Dive" or Challenge Email Sequences for Free Content Consumption

Instead of just offering a free trial, create highly personalized email sequences that offer a series of free, related Blinks on a specific topic the user showed interest in during onboarding or initial app usage.

We can challenge users to "work" for a great discount!

üí° Why: Provides instant value, builds habit, reduces friction to conversion by building trust and shows product value incrementally, using existing email infrastructure.

How:

We'll segment users by interest, track their progress through these "micro-courses," or "challenges" and identify who's most likely to convert. We can A/B test everything to optimize!

Challenges & Smart Solutions

Anticipating hurdles and building agile pathways to success

CRM CHALLENGE #1

"Too Much" Personalization

While personalization is key, going overboard can feel intrusive and lead to users opting out.

‚úÖ Solution: Intelligent Frequency Capping & Preference Centric

Smart Sending: We'll use AI to figure out the perfect number of communications for each user segment. If they're highly engaged, maybe more. If they're pulling back, we'll ease off

User Preference Centric: give users an easy-to-use preference center. They can choose what kind of messages they get, how often, and on what topics. This builds trust and ensures relevance

CRM CHALLENGE #2

Maintaining Brand Voice with AI

Using AI for content creation is powerful, but it might sometimes generate messages that don't sound like "Blinkist."

‚úÖ Solution: Clear Brand Guidelines & Human-in-the-Loop QA

Enhanced AI Brand Prompts: We'll feed the AI detailed brand guidelines, including our specific tone, vocabulary, and style examples

Human-in-the-Loop Quality Assurance: For our most important communications, a human will always review and approve the AI-generated content

NEW CHANNEL CHALLENGE #1 (Curated Email Sequences)

Content Overload and Maintaining Engagement Within Free Sequence

While offering free content is good, there's a risk of overwhelming users with too many emails or losing their interest if the content isn't compelling enough.

‚úÖ Solution: Smart Content Curation & Personalized Pacing

AI-Driven Content Matching: We'll use AI to pick the most engaging and relevant free Blinks for each user, making sure every email is high-value and personalized

Adaptive Pacing: Our system will adjust how quickly we send emails based on how users are engaging. If they're devouring Blinks, the next email comes sooner. If they're slow, we'll wait a bit

NEW CHANNEL CHALLENGE #2 (Interactive Content Widgets)

Maintaining Brand Control and Consistency Across Third-Party Sites

When Blinkist content is embedded or displayed on external sites, ensuring it maintains brand standards and provides a consistent user experience can be difficult.

‚úÖ Solution: Controlled API & Robust Brand Guidelines for Partners

Managed API & Content Delivery: We'll use a controlled system (an API) that directly delivers our content and branding (like logos and fonts) to partner sites. This way, it always looks consistent

Partner Onboarding & Guidelines: Develop clear, comprehensive guidelines for partners on on how to use our content and brand elements

Regular Checks: Regularly check partner sites to make sure they're following our brand guidelines
